About Bonita Event Planners

Turning ideas into unforgettable moments since 2018.

Our Story

Bonita Event Planners started as a small family-run business in Nairobi, organizing intimate birthday parties for friends and neighbors. Word of mouth grew quickly, and within a few years our team was coordinating weddings, corporate functions, and traditional ceremonies across the country. Today, we are a full-service event planning company built on the same principle we started with: every event, big or small, deserves careful attention and a personal touch.

Our Mission

To take the stress out of event planning by handling every detail from venue scouting and decor to catering coordination and on-the-day management, so our clients can focus on enjoying their own celebration.
